Abstract

We introduce EuraGovQA, a comprehensive multilingual multimodal benchmark designed to evaluate vision-language models on real-world government examination questions across Eurasian countries. Our benchmark comprises 15,234 questions spanning 17 subject areas from official civil service examinations in India, the European Union, Japan, Taiwan, and South Korea. Unlike existing benchmarks that focus primarily on English or academic contexts, EuraGovQA captures the diverse linguistic, cultural, and administrative knowledge required for government positions across different nations. We evaluate 13 state-of-the-art vision-language models and reveal significant performance gaps across languages and domains, highlighting the need for more culturally-aware multimodal AI systems.

Key Features

EuraGovQA provides a rigorous evaluation framework for assessing vision-language models on real-world governmental knowledge.

Multilingual

Questions in English, Hindi, Japanese, Traditional Chinese, and Korean, reflecting authentic examination formats.

Multimodal

Over 55% of questions include images such as diagrams, charts, maps, and figures essential for solving the problems.

Real Examinations

Sourced from actual government civil service examinations, ensuring practical relevance and difficulty calibration.

17 Subject Areas

Comprehensive coverage including Law, Medicine, Administration, Economics, Science, and Humanities.

Fine-grained Analysis

Detailed performance breakdown by country, language, subject, and question type for comprehensive evaluation.

Human Baseline

Includes expert human performance data collected from qualified professionals for meaningful comparison.
