EuraGovQA: A Multilingual Multimodal Benchmark for Government Examinations across Eurasia

Anonymous Authors

We introduce EuraGovQA, a comprehensive multilingual multimodal benchmark designed to evaluate vision-language models on real-world government examination questions across Eurasian countries. Our benchmark comprises 15,234 questions spanning 17 subject areas from official civil service examinations in India, the European Union, Japan, Taiwan, and South Korea.

Key Contributions

Large-scale Dataset

15,234 questions from real government examinations across 5 countries and 17 subject areas.

Comprehensive Evaluation

Systematic evaluation of 13 state-of-the-art vision-language models with fine-grained analysis.

Cultural Diversity

Questions in multiple languages reflecting authentic cultural and administrative contexts.

Multimodal Content

Over 55% of questions include images, diagrams, charts, and figures essential for solving.
